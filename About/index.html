What's this about cows and audiobooks?

“Looking for cows” is a metaphor—some days you want to leave it all and look for cattle in the hills, preferably by the ocean. It’s the desire to go search and discover. That’s why I created Cow’s Audiobooks.

Each series is a novella-sized original audio tale, written and narrated by me. Cows. Metaphorically it’s like standing out in a field, feeling the wind on your face while some cattle munch on grass in front of you.

A tiny audio escape so to speak. I even read some classic literature as a bonus. Such as “The Shepherd of the Hills.”

I hope you join me. Whenever you feel like it.
