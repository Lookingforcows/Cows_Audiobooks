Welcome to Cow's Audiobooks

Cow's Audiobooks is the storytelling podcast of M.p "Looking for Cows" Temple. Each audiobook is character-driven, thought-provoking (if you choose) with relatable quirky characters forced into ridiculous situations. From "Saku of the Skull Talkers" to "The Aurore Reset", to classics such as "Pride and Prejudice", each tale is brought to life through a unique and dramatic lens only cows could provide. So join in looking for cows, metaphorically speaking, in this anthology podcast. New seasons come out when the stories are ready. You can listen for free below!
