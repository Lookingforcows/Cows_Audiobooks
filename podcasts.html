Welcome to Cow's Audiobooks

Cow's Audiobooks is the storytelling podcast of M.p "Looking for Cows" Temple. Each audiobook is character-driven with relatable quirky characters forced into ridiculous situations. Each tale is brought to life through a unique and dramatic lens only cows could provide. From "Saku of the Skull Talkers" to "The Aurore Reset", to classics such as "Pride and Prejudice". So join in looking for cows, metaphorically speaking, in this anthology podcast. New seasons come out when the stories are ready. Listen and download for free!
